<h2><strong>Understanding Alkaline Water</strong></h2>
<h2>Alkaline water differs from regular tap water in its pH level. While standard water has a neutral pH of 7, alkaline water has a pH greater than 7, typically between 8 and 9. Proponents of alkaline water claim that it can help neutralize acid in the bloodstream, improve hydration, and provide antioxidant benefits.</h2>
